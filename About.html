The Hagerman Art Gallery

Artist exhibits are regularly displayed in the Hagerman Art Gallery, located in the Roland Fine Arts Center. The gallery is a learning tool for art students as they view the artwork and listen to artist presentations and Q&A sessions during exhibit opening receptions. A variety of artists and media are showcased in the gallery, each offering a cultural experience for the HLGU campus and Hannibal community.
